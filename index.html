Thomas Edwards

I am a PostDoc at Stockholm University working on Astroparticle and Gravitational Wave physics. I spend most of my time working on a combination of theoretical and computational methods to improve current analysis methods or enable searches for new physics. Get in contact or check out my code on Github!
